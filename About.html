ABOUT US

Engine Enhancers Automobile Solutions Private Limited (TASPL), a part of the $7.2 billion Engine Enhancers Group, is one of India’s largest players in independent automobile aftermarket business.
At myEngine Enhancers, customers can save up to 20 percent servicing costs as compared to authorized service centres. With the motto to provide right inspection, right parts and right billing, Car and Bike owners can now ‘Make the Right Choice’ by choosing our services to solve their woes.
Marching towards its vision, myEngine Enhancers is aggressively making inroads in the Indian car service market, which has historically been shared by OEM dealers and Independent Garages. The company aims to establish a country wide network of over 1000 workshops.
The organization have also entered the business of private label spare parts for all brands which stands for smart replacement and is targeted at customers who want quality and value for money spares for their cars.
